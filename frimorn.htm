Baby Time

August 6, 9:15 - 10:15AM

Babies (6 - 24 months)

With Miss Alex & Miss Jocelyn

Always wanted to try a program with your baby but weren't sure about the commitment? Unclear about expectations? Didn't know where to start in the first place?!?
Join Miss Jocelyn and Miss Alex for a fun, gentle, introduction to Baby Time, where we'll use songs, rhymes, bounces, and circle activities to get you and your little one into the swing of things! Class is 45 minutes long with a 15 minute 'Stay and Play' session afterwards -- You can chat with other parents over light refreshments while your children explore our tactile play area with each other!
