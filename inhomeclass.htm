At Home with Mother Goose

On Request

Babes to Walkers (0 - 24 months)

With Miss Valerie


'At Home with Mother Goose' is an 8-week group early literacy program offered in the comfort of your own home! Enjoy an hour of fun songs, tickles, and bounces, soothing lullabies, and engaging stories with your little one. We'll also discuss early literacy research and practices that will assist with language and communication development for your child.

All you need to do is gather together at least 5 of your friends and provide some open floor space (moving the couches against the wall in the TV room usually does the trick).

Dates and times are flexible
Sessions runs 8 weeks.
$100.00/Family

Request a Class

**Older siblings are welcome to attend as well, however the content of the program will be aimed at the infant level. Caregivers may wish to bring a doll or quiet toy for the older sibling to engage with during the program. Siblings are also welcome to participate, but the main focus will be on the younger child.
